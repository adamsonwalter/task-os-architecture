## Copy Formatting Conventions

### Headlines
- Sentence case or Title Case
- No ending punctuation (unless question)
- 6-12 words typical

### Body Copy
- Second person ("you" not "we")
- Short paragraphs (2-3 sentences)
- Scannable bullet points

### CTAs
- Action verb + outcome
- 2-5 words
- Sentence case or Title Case

### Testimonials
```
> "[Quote text]"
> — [Full Name], [Title] at [Company]
```
