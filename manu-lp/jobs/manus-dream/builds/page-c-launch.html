The Story

The dog who dared to dream at Old Trafford

Everyone says it's impossible. After all, dogs don't play football. But Manu has a secret weapon: belief.

Guided by a magical dream and a legacy passed down from his dad and grandad, Manu finds himself in the middle of a once-in-a-lifetime match — with the crowd roaring, his family behind him, and the goal just ahead.

Written by Paul B, a lifelong Manchester United fan whose love for the beautiful game came from his father, just as Manu's did. This is a story that only someone who truly understands what it means to support United could write.

Perfect for young readers aged 3–8. Poetic. Heartwarming. Built for the biggest stage — a child's imagination.
