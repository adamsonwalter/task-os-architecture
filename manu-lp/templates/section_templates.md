## Usage Notes

1. **Customization**: Replace all [bracketed] items with specific content
2. **Selection**: Choose templates that match the archetype from `ontology/conversion_archetypes.json`
3. **Combination**: Mix and match sections as needed
4. **Variants**: Generate 2-3 versions of key copy elements for A/B testing
5. **Validation**: Check completed sections against phase protocols
