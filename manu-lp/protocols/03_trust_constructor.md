# Phase 3: Trust Constructor Protocol

**Purpose**: Systematically build credibility through layered social proof  
**Value Equation Focus**: Perceived Likelihood of Achievement (↑↑↑)

---

## Trust Signal Taxonomy

### Tier 1: Highest Impact (use if available)
| Signal Type | Example | Trust Impact |
|-------------|---------|--------------|
| **Video testimonials** | Customer telling story on camera | +5 |
| **Specific case studies** | "Company X increased Y by Z%" | +5 |
| **Celebrity/Expert endorsement** | Known figure recommends | +4 |
| **Media logos** | "As seen in Forbes, TechCrunch" | +4 |

### Tier 2: High Impact
| Signal Type | Example | Trust Impact |
|-------------|---------|--------------|
| **Written testimonials with photo** | Quote + name + photo + title | +3 |
| **Client logos** | "Trusted by Google, Shopify..." | +3 |
| **Aggregate stats** | "50,000+ customers" | +3 |
| **Star ratings** | "★★★★★ 4.9/5 (2,500 reviews)" | +3 |

### Tier 3: Moderate Impact
| Signal Type | Example | Trust Impact |
|-------------|---------|--------------|
| **Trust badges** | SSL, GDPR, SOC2, ISO | +2 |
| **Payment logos** | Visa, Stripe, PayPal | +2 |
| **Guarantees** | "30-day money-back" | +2 |
| **Certifications** | Industry credentials | +2 |

### Tier 4: Foundational
| Signal Type | Example | Trust Impact |
|-------------|---------|--------------|
| **Live notifications** | "John from NYC just signed up" | +1 |
| **Counters** | "1,247 people viewing" | +1 |
| **Years in business** | "Since 2015" | +1 |

## Testimonial Optimization Formula

**Bad testimonial**:  
> "Great product, highly recommend!" — John

**Good testimonial** (follows STAR pattern):
> "Before [Product], I was [Situation]. I needed to [Task]. After using [Product], I [Action + Result]. I went from [metric before] to [metric after] in [timeframe]." — John Smith, CEO at Company (with photo)

### STAR Framework
- **S**ituation: Where were they before?
- **T**ask: What did they need to achieve?
- **A**ction: What did they do with your product?
- **R**esult: What specific outcome did they get?

## Testimonial Collection Prompts

If client lacks testimonials, provide collection scripts:

```
Email Template:
"Hi [Customer],

Quick favor that takes 2 minutes?

Could you answer these 3 questions about your experience with [Product]:

1. What was your biggest challenge before using [Product]?
2. What specific result have you achieved since using it?
3. Would you recommend it? Why?

Just reply to this email—I'll format it into a testimonial.

Thanks!
[Name]"
```

## Logo Bar Best Practices

**Arrangement**: 5-7 logos, grayscale, evenly spaced  
**Header options**:
- "Trusted by industry leaders"
- "Powering growth at"
- "Used by teams at"
- "Featured in"

**If no major logos**: Use industry categories
- "Trusted by 500+ marketing agencies"
- "Used by startups and Fortune 500s alike"

## If No Trust Assets Exist

**Immediate actions**:
1. Create a customer survey to collect testimonials
2. Screenshot any email praise/feedback
3. Compile usage statistics as social proof
4. Offer beta users incentive for detailed reviews
5. Get founder's personal network for initial testimonials
